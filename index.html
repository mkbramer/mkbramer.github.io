MK 🦇 Bramer

I am a 20 year old VCD major with a minor in Creative Writing. I live with my partner and 2 cats, Bucky & Goomba.

Origin Story

I am the art child of my family. For Christmas I received the cheap art kits with the markers that dried out too fast. I always loved art, drawing, and most of all, stories. In my sophomore year of college I went through a quarter life that almost WWE body slammed me into academic suspension and left me without a clue of what I was going to do with my life.

SUPER POWER

My ability to hyper-focus on a story. From movies to books to my own original characters.
